Capstone Project

Capstone Project 1 and 2 (Thesis)

Mosquinator: A Community-Based Mobile and Web Application Framework for combating Dengue and providing Awareness for Residents of Pasig City

This study is about a community-oriented web and mobile application that will advocate a practical approach to providing a platform for reporting concerns and the exchange of information between the Pasig Dengue Task Force and the residents of Pasig City about dengue-related problems or incidents, as well as promoting awareness and knowledge regarding mosquito-borne diseases for the residents.

This project was developed by our team, Innovation Geeks, which consists of a four-member group of IT students specializing in Mobile and Web Applications under the guidance of Prof. Leonila B. Valdez, a faculty member of the College of Computing and Information Technologies at the National University Philippines - Manila (Main Campus).
